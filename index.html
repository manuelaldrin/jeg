About Us

Welcome to JEG Development Corporation

JEG Tower At One Acacia is the first of its kind in Cebu City. The iconic property is JEG Development Corporation’s pioneering office and retail high-rise building that aims to revolutionize work and life balance. As a trailblazing structure, the tower’s masterful architectural design was entrusted to the renowned firm, Casas Architecture.

The project is also a LEED pre-certified silver building that boasts of resource efficient quality in design and construction. True to its commitment to quality and the environment, JEG Tower features state-of-the-art facilities with 100% back-up power, VRF air conditioning and 24/7 capability. The structure has enhanced interior spaces for maximum views and natural lighting and carries landscaped green spaces along with other energy-saving amenities in the form of bike racks and water-conserving shower rooms.
